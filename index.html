Hi I'm Jessie! I'm a digital media artist that specializes in illustration and 3d modeling. I love to create cute things but I'm not hesitant to go outside of my usual aesthetic and try something new. I really love anime and cartoons so a lot of my work is influenced by them. I began my digital art journey in 2016 and originally wanted to major in animation. I ended up switching over to digital media art (DMA) and it was one of the best decisions I've ever made. In DMA, I was able to learn so many things, including animation. I developed a love for creating 3d artworks which was something I didn't expect to even like at first. I graduated from San Jose State University with my Bachelors and I'm now looking to expand and hone my artistic skills.

3D Projects

Feelin' Snackish

Video Demo
Feelin’ Snackish is a 3d virtual installation based on two snacks from childhood: White Rabbit Candy and Dried Squid. I wanted to use flat and simple shapes and colors to evoke a childlike aesthetic. I created my own material textures for the candies and the dried squid snack bags. I deployed a VR version of this on a Oculus Quest headset which I displayed during ArtOfficial, at the Hammer Theatre Center, San Jose, CA.
Created with: Blender, Unity, Procreate, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator

The Nuggets


Video Demo
The Nuggets is a group collaborative project where each of our team members recreated some of our favorite films but as if they were filmed with chickens! Everyone used my chicken model as the base for their characters. My team was inspired by cartoons and comic humor. Movie worlds were based off of Blade Runner, My Neighbor Totoro, Howl's Moving Castle, and Turning Red. I created the Totoro world (3:00 in Video Demo) and based it off of the iconic rainy bus stop scene in the movie.
Created with: Blender, Unity

Chimkens in Space

A small, fun project that introduced me to Mozilla Hubs. This is a fictional world of a few chickens having to survive in space after Earth has, well, gotten destroyed.
Created with: Blender, Mozilla Hubs
